About

Hello! My name is Maliha Islam and I am a software engineer and artist from Queens, New York. I am passionate about the intersections between tech and society - the ways in which technological innovation has changed the way we connect with others, and how the needs of the people reflect in the way technology is developed. Nearly all of my time is spent leaning over a laptop, but I enjoy creating, designing, and producing even on my own free time. I also advocate heavily within community organizing and activism spaces.
