Omena Madagascar

Description

Omena Madagascar is an organization promoting mental health education in the US and Madagascar. The initiative is primarily youth-led, with focus on developing content trainings and spreading awareness of emotional abuse.

Design Work

I played a pivotal role in the organization during its developmental stages as a content creator and designer. This meant being involved in the research and development of educational material pertaining to emotional regulation and abuse and finding creative ways to communicate to a wider audience. I worked alongside several other content creators and compiled all research into beautifully-designed graphics meant to engage those interested in Omena's message. These graphics served as templates for social media posts, which were then filled in with information and infographics and posted on Instagram.

Content Package Designs

In addition to social media content, I was also charged with designing training packages for facilitators as well as students for our emotional learning courses.
