Develop for Good

My Roles

Through Develop for Good's 6 month program, I worked alongside a team of 5 other developers to redesign a website for Neuro Diverse Living, a nonprofit organization providing housing for autistic individuals with higher assistance needs.

Designer

  • Established a style guide using existing colors and typography on the website
  • Consulted with the CEO of the organization to recognize their vision and highlight their priority for emphasizing donations
  • Developed a high fidelity prototype on Figma and underwent multiple rounds of approval

Developer

  • Translated Figma design into usable product using SASS-powered CSS and Bootstrap-powered HTML
  • Optimized SEO for website to generate more traffic
  • Developed a scalable, responsive design and ensured seamless project handover after development period
